The 3rd Annual Arizona Feminist Philosophy Graduate Conference will be held on March 26-27, hosted by the Department of Philosophy at the University of Arizona. The conference will take place with an all hybrid in-person/Zoom format.

Our FemPhilAZ 2022 keynote speakers will be Robin Dembroff (Assistant Professor of Philosophy, Yale University) and Eva Kittay (Distinguished Professor of Philosophy, SUNY Stony Brook).

The conference is organized by Anna-Bella Sicilia, Luke Golemon, Karolina Wisniewska, Ding, Rhys Borchert, Max Kramer, April Chan, Jacob Blitz, and Ritwik Agrawal.
