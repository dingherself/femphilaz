The 5th Annual Arizona Feminist Philosophy Graduate Conference will be held on April 5–7, 2024, hosted by the Department of Philosophy at the University of Arizona. The conference will take place in Tucson, Arizona with a hybrid Zoom option for all talks.

Our FemPhilAZ 2024 keynote speakers will be Nora Berenstain (University of Tennessee, Knoxville) and Amy Reed-Sandoval (University of Nevada, Las Vegas).

This year’s conference is organized by Anna-Bella Sicilia, Luke Golemon, Ding, Kyle Kirby, and Jacob Blitz.
