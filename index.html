The Arizona Feminist Philosophy Graduate Conference is organized every spring by graduate students at the University of Arizona Philosophy Department.

We are excited to announce that FemPhilAZ 2025 will be held on February 7–8, 2025, in an all-hybrid format as usual and with Professor A. W. Eaton (University of Illinois Chicago) and Professor Rowan Bell (University of Guelph) as our keynote speakers.

While we have now moved on to reading submissions, please feel free to continue to reach out to us if you are interested in being considered as a commentator or chair (please see instructions in our call for papers linked below).
