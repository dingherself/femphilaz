The Arizona Feminist Philosophy Graduate Conference is organized every spring by graduate students at the University of Arizona Philosophy Department, now in its sixth year.

FemPhilAZ 2025 will be held on February 7–8, 2025, in an all-hybrid format as usual and with Professor A.W. Eaton (feminist philosophy of art, University of Illinois Chicago) and Professor Rowan Bell (trans feminist philosophy, University of Guelph) as our keynote speakers.
