2022 Conference Program

The conference will take place with an all hybrid in-person/Zoom format. Our in-person venue will be the Maloney Seminar Room, Social Sciences Building Room 224 (1145 E South Campus Dr).

Zoom participants: Please note that Tucson does not observe Daylight Saving Time. All times listed below are in Mountain Standard Time (MST), which is not the same as Mountain Time (MT) or Mountain Daylight Time (MDT)!

Sponsors: We would like to thank all of our 2022 conference sponsors for making FemPhilAZ 2022 possible.
